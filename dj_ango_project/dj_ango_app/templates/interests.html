Interests

I am an accomplished musician, principally a guitarist but also vocalist, bassist and drummer. I have recorded and performed live professionally both solo and with a number of bands in Glasgow, Edinburgh, Perth and Aberdeenshire, as well as further afield in New York, Canada and South America whilst travelling.

I am also an accomplished Spanish speaker with an interest in the food and culture of Spain and the Spanish diaspora. I previously took out some time between 3rd and 4th year at university to travel across Latin America.
